    • About TinDev

    At its core, TinDev is meant to disrupt and innovate the recruiting process. It aims to facilitate the process of finding the perfect match for a technical position, whether it is a Software Engineering, Quality Engineering, or Full Stack Developer positions. TinDev makes it easy for any candidate wanting to find a job to register and be able to see open positions and demonstrate interest, whereas recruiters can also see qualified candidates within the area. We provice a product whose emphasis is on ease of use, whose navigation is straightforward, thus revolutionizing the professional scene and the area of recruiting forever.

    Current popular technical positions: Software Engineering Quality Engineering Full Stack Developer Web Developer UX Designer IT Product Manager


    Our application is home to some of the most amazing technical recruiters and candidates from around the globe, and our mission is to give them the platform to connect in the simplest possible way! From having job postings and compability checks to even demonstrating interest and extending/receiving actual offers, TinDev has it all!


    Remember, out motto is simple:


    Connecting the world's smartest people, through the world's smartest platform.
